Our Mission

Our goal at Resque Radar is to bring together the unsung heroes of different rescue organizations under one strong roof, becoming a formidable force committed to offering quick, dependable, and life-saving reactions during emergencies. When a disaster happens, we think that relief and assistance should be given right away. Every moment counts because lives are on the line. We have therefore set out to coordinate the operations of the rescue organizations, bringing them all together to create a strong network of first responders. Our organization acts as the lighthouse that directs various organizations, making sure they are organized and prepared to effectively manage any emergency crisis.
